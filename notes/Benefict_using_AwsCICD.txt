Benefits of Using This AWS CI/CD Stack:
**************************************

        1- Full AWS integration: IAM roles, S3, CloudWatch, CloudTrail.
           ******************** 

        2- Automatic rollbacks: CodeDeploy can revert failed deployments.
           *******************

        3- Serverless orchestration: No need to maintain Jenkins/other CI servers.
           ************************

        4- Scalable: Works with large deployments, multi-AZ EC2 clusters, ECS, or Lambda.
           ********

        5- Custom hooks: Pre/post deployment scripts for configuration.
           ************
